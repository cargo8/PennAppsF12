Shortcodes

Easy to remember shortcodes give our users the ability to share moments, pictures, and thoughts with their contacts like never before:

  • r# - List of recipients to share with
  • * - Reply * or click to 'Star' your friend's post
  • l# - A particularly interesting link
  • v# - A funny embeddable video!
  • pb# - A public share that might be seen by any of your contacts on Emailr
  • pr# - A public post with private replies only
